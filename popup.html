How to train your dargon
  1. Brew a shot of espresso (about 30ml).
  2. Steam and froth 60–90ml of milk until creamy.
  3. Pour the milk over the espresso.
  4. Top with milk foam and sprinkle cocoa powder if desired.

Tip: Use fresh coffee beans for the best flavor!
